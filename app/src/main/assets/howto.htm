How To Use

After assigning an app to a slot, you need to manually drag the button to your active Quick Settings in your Notifications panel to enable it.

If the black & white app icon looks weird, tap the image and adjust the slider to get a better image.

Note:
The shortcut buttons will be labelled as (Add & close Edit to refresh) while they are in the inactive area.
This is a quirk of the Quick Settings shortcut system (and I dunno how to change this for now).
The shortcut name and icon will be updated when it is dragged to the active area and the Edit menu closed.

Adding A Button To Quick Settings

  1. From the top of your screen, swipe down twice.
  2. At the bottom corner, tap Edit .
  3. Touch and hold the setting. Then drag the setting to where you want it.
    • To add a setting, drag it up from "Hold and drag to add tiles."
    • To remove a setting, drag it down to "Drag here to remove."

Reference: "Add, remove, or move a setting" (support.google.com)
